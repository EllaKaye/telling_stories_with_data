A.2 Shoulders of giants

Chapter 1:

Shoulders of giants Dr Michael Jordan is Pehong Chen Distinguished Professor at the University of California, Berkeley. After taking a PhD in Cognitive Science from University of California, San Diego, in 1985, he was appointed as an assistant professor at MIT, being promoted to full professor in 1997, and in 1998 he moved to Berkeley. One area of his research is statistical machine learning. One particularly important paper is Blei, Ng, and Jordan (2003), which enables text to be grouped together to define topics.
